Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Discount Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 No Personal Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. This includes:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or unique user IDs
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2.2 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your preferred discount percentages
  • Your preferred tax rates
  • Calculation history (stored locally only)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2.3 Advertising Data

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • App usage data
  • General location information (if location services are enabled)

This data collection is handled by Google AdMob and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this information.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use personal data for any purposes. The locally stored preferences are used solely to:

  • Remember your preferred discount and tax settings
  • Improve your user experience within the App

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any personal information because we do not collect any. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Google AdMob: Advertising data is handled by Google according to their privacy policy
  • No Third Parties: We do not share data with any other third parties

5. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, we rely on your device's built-in security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device lock screens and security features
  • Being cautious about granting permissions to other apps

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear the App's local data by uninstalling and reinstalling the App
  • Disable advertising tracking in your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Including a notice in future App updates
