Privacy Policy for TinySpark Apps

Last updated: December 2024

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data to improve your experience across all our apps. Your preferences and usage patterns help us make our apps better. We don't sell your data to third parties.

Our Apps

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by TinySpark Apps, including:

  • Hindu Baby Names with Meaning - Baby name discovery and meaning app
  • Future Apps - Any additional apps we may release under this developer account

1. Information We Collect

1.1 App-Specific Data Collection

Hindu Baby Names with Meaning:

  • Favorite Names: Names you save to your favorites list
  • Recently Viewed Names: Names you've viewed to show in your history
  • Search Queries: Names you search for (stored locally on your device)
  • Usage Statistics: How many names you view, favorites you add (for review prompts)

Future Apps:

Each future app may collect different types of data. We will update this privacy policy to specify the data collection practices for each new app before its release.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's functionality
  • To show you recently viewed names
  • To save your favorite names list
  • To occasionally prompt for app reviews (after you've used the app meaningfully)
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For app analytics and crash reporting (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Google Play Services: For in-app reviews and Play Store integration

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • Usage statistics are stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • We don't store personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers

5. Your Rights

  • Delete Data: You can clear your favorites and recently viewed names anytime in the app
  • Opt Out: You can disable analytics in your device settings
  • Data Export: You can export your favorites list through the app's share feature

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for parents choosing baby names. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
